POETRY

MEDITATIONS ON DELIBERATIONS

poetry on thoughts on actions

Cory's first published book, meditations on deliberations is a poetry collection covering a range of topics from midwestern corn, pineapples, art, science, poetry, and death. The collection was written chronologically spanning Cory's life from high school through college graduation and into early adulthood. Meditations on deliberations is available for purchase on Amazon.
