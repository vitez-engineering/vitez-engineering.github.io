Vitez Engineering
About Us
Vitez Engineering was founded to reduce cost and risk associated with developing and flying GNC algorithms. We are working toward a future where GNC flight software is low cost, low risk, and turnkey by developing GNC algorithms and models that are highly extensible to any form factor of satellite. From GEO sat to nanosat, our product simply works out of the box to meet ADCS and GNC system performance requirements, or help define requirements with high fidelity simulations.
Our Leadership
Cory Vitez graduated from Purdue University in Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering. He has experience from across the aerospace industry in both Vehicle Software Engineering and Guidance, Navigation, and Control Engineering. His years of experience include time at NASA, Firefly Aerospace, Numerica Corporation, Slingshot Aerospace, and ABL Space Systems. Most recently, Cory has shifted his focus to Vitez Engineering, a company founded to reduce cost and risk associated with developing and flying GNC algorithms.
