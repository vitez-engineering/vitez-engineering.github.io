ABOUT

MISSION

orbital precision, orbital speed

Vitez Engineering was founded to reduce cost and risk associated with developing and flying GNC algorithms. We are working toward a future where GNC flight software and simulation is plug and play by developing GNC algorithms and models that are highly extensible to any form factor of satellite or vehicle. From GEO sat to nanosat, our tools simply works out of the box to meet ADCS and GNC system performance requirements.
Vitez Engineering provides flight software that simply works right away, with all the GNC capabilities to fly your mission. With access to ultra high fidelity satellite simulations from day zero, we make component sizing and selection a piece of cake. Our simulations offer ultra high fidelity models of the entire space domain. Accurate simulation is an integral part of mission success, which is why our simulation suite is rigorously tested to the same standard as our flight software.

LEADERSHIP

Cory Vitez graduated from Purdue University in Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering, where he won the Outstanding Aerospace Engineer Award. He has experience from across the aerospace industry in Guidance, Navigation, and Control Engineering. He has worked cradle-to-grave on engineering rockets, satellites, and re-entry vehicles. His years of experience include time at NASA, Firefly Aerospace, ABL Space Systems, and Varda Space Industries. In his free time, Cory enjoys hiking, biking, photography, and writing poetry.
