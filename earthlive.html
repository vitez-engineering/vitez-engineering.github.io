Foundation Models for Physical AI

Current computer vision models are bottlenecked by static, two-dimensional training data. EarthLive’s continuous video telemetry provides a high-temporal-resolution pipeline of planetary movement. By streaming the real-time flow of traffic, maritime logistics, and natural phenomena, we enable developers to train next-generation spatial AI that understands physics, causality, and complex temporal dynamics at a global scale. We aren't just observing the earth; we are building the training set to model it.

Disaster Response & Recovery

Static satellite photos are historical data. EarthLive provides live video of advancing wildfire fronts, active flood crests, and post-seismic damage. We enable emergency coordinators to route life-saving resources based on what is happening now, not what happened six hours ago.

Tactical Intelligence & Defense

Eliminate the tasking latency of traditional orbital assets. EarthLive provides a continuous stream of tactical intelligence, allowing for the real-time tracking of high-value assets across contested or remote environments without the need for vulnerable ground-based sensors or high-altitude drones.

Commodity & Economic Alpha

Measure the heartbeat of the physical economy. Track heavy machinery activity at mines, loading rates at bulk terminals, cars in front of buisnesses, and vehicle throughput at logistics hubs. By capturing real-time comercial and industrial telemetry at planetary scale, EarthLive provides institutional investors with data before it hits the news cycle.

Infrastructure & Urban Flow

Monitor the progress of mega-projects and the efficiency of urban transit systems. High-refresh video allows for the analysis of traffic bottlenecks and construction milestones across entire metropolitan areas simultaneously, providing civil engineers and developers with unprecedented data density.

Maritime Domain Awareness

Detect "dark ships" and monitor global chokepoints with persistent optical tracking. Our sub-meter resolution allows for the identification of vessel classes and the monitoring of ship-to-ship transfers in international waters, providing a continuous audit trail for global security and sanctions enforcement.

Environmental Enforcement

Illegal logging, unauthorized mining, and oil spills often occur in the blind spots of traditional monitoring. EarthLive provides a persistent eye in the sky, ensuring that environmental violations are documented in high-definition as they occur, providing undeniable evidence for legal and regulatory action.
